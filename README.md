Vehicle Service Management System:

   A Vehicle Service Management System developed using **Python** and **MySQL** to automate and manage vehicle service center operations.

Project Overview:

   The Vehicle Service Management System (VSMS) is a console-based application designed to replace manual record-keeping in vehicle service centers.  
It helps manage customer details, vehicle records, service history, and billing efficiently using a centralized database.

This system reduces paperwork, improves data accuracy, and enhances customer service quality.

Objectives:

 1.To automate vehicle service operations
 2.To maintain digital records of customers and vehicles
 3.To generate automatic billing invoices
 4.To reduce manual errors
 5.To improve inventory and service tracking

Features:

 1.User Login Authentication
 2.Add & View Customers
 3.Vehicle Registration
 4.Service Entry & Billing
 5.Automatic Invoice Generation
 6.Service Record Management
 7.MySQL Database Integration
